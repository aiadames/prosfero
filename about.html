About Us

prosféro provides a platform for artists to sell their artwork to clients who donate to charities, non-profits, and other organizations which the artist supports and would like to use their talents to assist in the cause.

Mission Statement

prosféro is a way for artists to turn their art into charitable contributions. In an era of activism, artists are at the forefront of providing their invaluable services for a way to promote societal change.

Vision Statement

prosféro aspires to transform the way in which artists can contribute their talents to activism and altruism. Through our website, we hope that artists will be able to follow their passion for art, improve upon their skills while contributing to causes they find important, and connect with future clients from all around the world.
